Chicken Adobo

Description

This is a classic Filipino dish traditionally served with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 whole garlic bulb, smashed and peeled
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 pounds bone-in chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to pot and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat chicken in skillet.
