Chicken Tinola

Description

This is a classic Filipino dish traditionally served with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 piece fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 lbs chicken legs and thighs
  • 2 cans chicken broth
  • 1 chayote squash
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 head bok choy
  • 1 package fresh spinach
  1. Heat oil in pan and add chicken
  2. Pour in chicken broth
  3. Add bok choy and spinach
  4. Cook for 40 minutes
