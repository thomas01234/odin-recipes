Sinigang

Description

This is a classic Filipino dish traditionally served with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs pork ribs
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 packet sinigant tamarind soup mix
  • 16 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
  • 1 bok choy
  • 1 daikon radish
  • 1 small tomato
  • 2 small potatoes

Steps

  1. Saute ribs, garlic, onions, and salt
  2. In large pot, add water and soup mix
  3. Add tomato and pork
  4. Cook for 40 minutes
